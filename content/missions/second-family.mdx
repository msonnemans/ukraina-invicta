The second family to have been helped by UI fled from the Donetsk region: Yevhen, Maryna, Veronica and Polina. Yevhen was able to escape due to his disability, along with his two daughters and Maryna who is expecting. They are currently being sponsored thanks to Aaron as he was able to secure sponsorship for their stay in Poland. The generous sponsor family from the United States has already send funds and are in contact via email.
